Index Fund Management Fees

Index funds are designed to passively track a market index, resulting in significantly lower management overhead compared to actively managed funds. Consequently, the average expense ratio for an index mutual fund typically ranges between 0.05% and 0.20% annually. Some highly competitive funds may offer expense ratios as low as 0.00% or 0.015%.
